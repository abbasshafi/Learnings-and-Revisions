##Zero-Shot Prompting:
You are prompting the model for a response without examples of the task you want to achieve. It depends upone the complexity and knowledge of task at hand.

#Example prompt:
-<Question>?
-what is prompt engineering?

##Few-Shot Prompting:
 one popular and effective technique to prompting is referred to as few-shot prompting where you provide examples.

#Example prompt:
-<Question>?
<Answer>

<Question>?
<Answer>

<Question>?
<Answer>

-This is awesome! // Positive
This is bad! // Negative
Wow that movie was rad! // Positive
What a horrible show! //


Few-shot prompts enable in-context learning, which is the ability of language models to learn tasks given a few demonstrations.
